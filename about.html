Our Story

Who We Are

IWear began with a simple mission: to provide affordable, stylish eyewear that doesn't compromise on quality. What started as a small shop in Manila has grown into a beloved eyewear brand with customers throughout the Philippines.

Our Mission

To provide everyone with access to high-quality, stylish eyewear that empowers them to express their unique personality while maintaining optimal eye health.

Our Values

Quality First

We never compromise on the quality of our frames, lenses, or customer service.

Affordable Style

We believe everyone deserves stylish eyewear without breaking the bank.

Customer Care

Your satisfaction and eye health are our top priorities.
